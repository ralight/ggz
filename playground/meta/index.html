General Information

The site meta.ggzgamingzone.org runs ggzmetaserv, the GGZ metaserver. It operates on port 15689 and lists all permanent ggzd's, that is GGZ servers, known to us. Along with the URI, auxiliary information is made available like the existence of a community portal. This web page is static; no metaserver queries are possible through the web at the time being. Please use a client like Plasma-KGGZ in KDE 4 to access the metaserver.

This metaserver is operated by the GGZ Gaming Zone and aggregated on the Metacle
