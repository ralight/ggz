GGZ Games

 There are currently 7 games available: TicTacToe, NetSpades, LaPocha, Connect the Dots, Hastings1066, Reversi and Combat. In the future this number will grow, and so will the number of players too, so be sure to learn how to play all the games before the others do!

TicTacToe by Brent Hendricks, ?? 2000
Nothing much to say. Cross against circle, PC agains human. Your goal ist to win on the 3x3 field by placing three symbols in one row.

NetSpades by Brent Hendricks, ?? 2000
A cards game which can be played with 4 players, each human or AI.

LaPocha by Rich Gade, ?? 2000
Another cards game with different rules, and a guarantee for long game sessions.

Connect the Dots by Rich Gade, ??
This makes addictive: Try to catch most of the squares.

Hastings1066 by Josef Spillner, September 2000
All ancient European kingdoms built upon this battle.

Reversi by Ismael Orenstein, September 2000
Your goal is to turn around most of your opponent's stones, turning their color to your one.
