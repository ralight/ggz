!! IMPORTANT !!
Only 'add' works currently, 'delete' and 'modify' are not implemented yet!!!

Add an entry to GGZ:

<?xml version="1.0"?><update class="ggz" type="connection" username="josef" password="ggzrocks"><option name="mode">add</option><option name="version">0.0.5pre</option><option name="preference">50</option><option name="uri">ggz://somehost.org:5688</option></update>

Remove an entry from GGZ:

<?xml version="1.0"?><update class="ggz" type="connection" username="josef" password="ggzrocks"><option name="mode">delete</option><option name="version">0.0.5pre</option><option name="preference">50</option><option name="uri">ggz://somehost.org:5688</option></update>

Add an entry to Atlantik:

<?xml version="1.0"?><update class="atlantik" type="connection" username="capsi" password="whatever"><option name="mode">add</option><option name="version">0.0.5pre</option><option name="preference">50</option><option name="uri">atlantik://someotherhost.org:9999</option></update>

Query GGZ:

<?xml version="1.0"?><query class="ggz" type="connection">0.0.5pre</query>

Query Altantik:

<?xml version="1.0"?><query class="atlantik" type="connection">0.1</query>
